## Student Achievements – NPTEL Online Certification (Programming in Java)

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering proudly congratulates the following students for successfully completing the **NPTEL Online Certification – Programming in Java (12 Weeks)**, conducted by **IIT Kharagpur** through the **SWAYAM** platform.

The students demonstrated exceptional academic excellence by securing **Elite Certification** with outstanding consolidated scores ranging from **90% to 93%**. Their achievements reflect strong programming fundamentals, consistent learning, and commitment to enhancing their technical competencies beyond the regular curriculum.

These certifications strengthen students' knowledge in Java programming, object-oriented programming concepts, exception handling, multithreading, collections, file handling, and software development principles, preparing them for higher studies, internships, coding competitions, and industry careers.
